## What Affects the Final Price

### 1. Wall Type

Drywall is the standard — no surcharge. But **brick, stone, concrete, tile, and metal** require masonry bits and specialized anchors, which is why there is a $25 surcharge. The installation is just as clean and secure.

### 2. Cable Concealment

Loose cables hanging down a wall are the most common complaint after a DIY mount. Professional [cable concealment](/cable-concealment-nashville) routes all wires in-wall or through a slim painted raceway for $60 per TV. Most customers who see the result say it was worth every dollar.

### 3. Fireplace Installs

Mounting above a fireplace adds complexity: we verify heat clearance, choose the right height, and route cables along a firplace wall. This starts at a $25 surcharge. Read more in our [TV mounting over fireplace guide](/tv-mounting-over-fireplace-nashville).

### 4. TV Size

Large TVs (60"+) require heavier mounts, longer bolts, and often a two-person team to hang safely. That is reflected in the higher base rate.

## Is Professional Mounting Worth It vs. DIY?

A wall anchor that fails with a 65" TV hanging on it causes $1,000+ in damage and a real safety hazard. Professional mounting takes 45–75 minutes and includes a workmanship warranty. Most Nashville homeowners who have tried both say the cost difference is minimal and the peace of mind is not.
